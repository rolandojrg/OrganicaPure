Are there any OrganicaPure products containing alcohol?

The only OrganicaPure product that contains alcohol is the Body Splash from the Cocoa & Honey line. We used food-grade ethly alcohol (ethanol) developed from grain, fruit or vegetables. The way our Body Splash is formulated avoids any side effects of dry skin due to a careful development process to obtain the final product. In the future, if there's any other products containing alcohol, it will be listed here.

I am allergic to peanuts. Should I be concerned about the nutshell used in OrganicaPure's products?

The nutshells used in some of our products (i.e. Body Scrubs) are meant to be used as a natural organic exfoliants for your skin. THey contain no nut oils of any kind. If you're unsure about the reaction you may get, please double check with your health prodvider about this matter
